# AutoFlow Brand Positioning & Visual Direction

## Core Essence
AutoFlow is the **intelligent nervous system** for SMBs and Developer teams. It is **AI-native, MCP-standard, and BYOLLM-ready**.

## Target Audience
- **The Pragmatic Developer:** Needs technical credibility, code-level control, and MCP compliance.
- **The AI-First SMB Owner:** Needs modern polish, fast time-to-value, and intelligent defaults.

## Visual Archetype: "The Electric Lab"
We sit at the intersection of **Laboratory Precision** (Technical/Scientific) and **Electric Speed** (Modern/Digital).

### 1. Color System: "High-Contrast Logic"
- **Primary:** `AutoFlow Indigo` (`#6366f1`) - Trustworthy but modern.
- **Accents:** 
  - `Electric Teal` (`#14b8a6`) - Symbolizing AI execution.
  - `Cyber Orange` (`#f97316`) - Symbolizing human intervention/triggers.
- **Base:** `Obsidian Dark` (`#0f172a`) - Our primary surface is Dark Mode.

### 2. Typography: "Technical Humanism"
- **Headings:** `Geist` or `Inter` (Tight tracking, bold weights) - Modern, clean, developer-trusted.
- **Body:** `Inter` (High legibility).
- **Code/Data:** `JetBrains Mono` or `Geist Mono`.

### 3. Iconography & Tiles: "The MCP Standard"
- **The Tile:** A rounded rectangle (`radius-lg`) with a subtle glassmorphism or border-glow effect.
- **Logo Treatment:** Original integration logos preserved but housed in a canonical "AutoFlow Frame" to ensure consistency on the canvas.
- **Skill Identity:** Each skill gets a 2-tone icon (Primary Indigo + Accent Teal).

### 4. Motion & Interactivity: "Fluid Logic"
- **Transitions:** Snappy but fluid (e.g., `ease-in-out`, `duration-200`).
- **Feedback:** "Pulse" effects for active AI thinking. "Spark" effects for successful connections.
- **Canvas:** Smooth zooming, snapping to grid, "Spring" physics for node connections.

### 5. Dashboard UX Patterns: "Prompt to Flow"
- **Interface:** A hybrid of a "Command Center" and a "Creative Studio".
- **AI Integration:** Prompts should be visually distinct (e.g., `Cyber Orange` glow) from static configuration.

## Mood Boards (Conceptual)
1. **The Orchestrator:** Focus on complex graphs made beautiful (Dark mode, neon traces).
2. **The Assistant:** Focus on human-AI collaboration (Chat bubbles, glowing tiles, clean typography).
3. **The Infrastructure:** Focus on stability and precision (Grid patterns, monospace overlays, terminal-inspired stats).
