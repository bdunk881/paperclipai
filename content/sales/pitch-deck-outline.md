# AutoFlow Pitch Deck Outline

## Slide 1: Title
- **AutoFlow** — AI-Native Workflow Automation
- Tagline: "Describe it. AutoFlow builds it."

## Slide 2: The Problem
- Teams spend 30%+ of their time on repetitive manual tasks
- Existing automation tools (Zapier, Make) require technical setup and rigid rule-based logic
- Workflows break when conditions change, requiring constant maintenance
- Complex multi-step automations need developer involvement

## Slide 3: The Solution
- AutoFlow uses AI to understand natural language workflow descriptions
- Builds, tests, and deploys automations from plain English instructions
- Self-healing: adapts to edge cases without manual intervention
- No coding or technical expertise required

## Slide 4: How It Works
1. **Describe** — Tell AutoFlow what you want automated in plain English
2. **Build** — AI constructs the workflow, selects integrations, and handles logic
3. **Deploy** — One-click deployment with real-time monitoring
4. **Adapt** — AutoFlow learns from patterns and improves over time

## Slide 5: Key Features
- Natural language workflow builder
- AI-powered routing and decision-making
- Multi-step orchestration across 100+ integrations
- Self-healing automations with automatic error recovery
- Real-time monitoring and analytics dashboard

## Slide 6: Use Cases
- **Customer Support:** Auto-route tickets, generate responses, escalate intelligently
- **Sales Operations:** Lead scoring, CRM updates, follow-up sequencing
- **Finance:** Invoice processing, expense approvals, reconciliation
- **HR:** Onboarding workflows, leave management, document processing
- **Marketing:** Campaign orchestration, content distribution, analytics reporting
